BaseballLeague User Story:

As the user, I want to create a new baseball team with a name.
As the user, I want to create a new player associated with a team.
As the user, I want to see a list of all the teams in the league.
As the user, I want to see a list of all the players on a particular team.
As the user, I want to move a player from one team to another team.
As the user, I want to remove a player from a team by name.
